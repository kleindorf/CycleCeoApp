Frequently asked questions

How does Cycle CEO know my cycle phase?

You enter the start date of your last period and your typical cycle length during setup. The app uses this to estimate which phase you're in (Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulatory, or Luteal) and tailors your schedule accordingly. These are estimates, not medical predictions.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your device. Cycle CEO does not use accounts or cloud servers, and your information is never uploaded. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I add my businesses and projects?

During setup, or any time from the Projects tab, you can add a project with its priority, weekly hour budget, and individual tasks. You can tag each task to a specific cycle phase so it appears at the right time.

How do I get my schedule into my calendar?

On any schedule block you can tap to add it to Google Calendar, or export a full day or week as a calendar file that imports into Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook.

I changed my projects but my schedule looks the same.

Tap the Regenerate button on the Today screen to rebuild the day's schedule with your latest projects and tasks.

How do I delete my data?

You can edit or remove your information inside the app at any time. Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.
